# Unit Economics - Credex AI Spend Audit

## Business Model
The tool is a **Lead Generation Magnet**. The goal is not direct monetization of the audit, but conversion into high-ticket AI consulting or implementation services.

## Cost Per Audit (CPA)
- **Anthropic API**: ~$0.005 per audit (using Claude 3 Haiku).
- **Hosting (Vite/Supabase)**: $0 (Free tier for initial scale).
- **Marketing**: $0 (Organic/GTM focus).

## Value Per Lead (VPL)
- **Consulting Engagement**: $10,000 - $50,000.
- **Conversion Rate (Audit -> Lead)**: Estimated 15%.
- **Conversion Rate (Lead -> Sale)**: Estimated 2%.
- **Effective VPL**: (10,000 * 0.02) = $200.

## ROI Analysis
With a cost per audit of <$0.01 and a value per lead of ~$200, the ROI on this tool is extremely high, assuming targeted traffic.

## Savings for Users
The primary value prop is **immediate ROI** for the user.
- **Average Audit Saving**: $250 - $1,200/mo.
- **Payback Period for User**: Immediate.
