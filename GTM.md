# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: CTOs and VPs of Engineering at Series A+ startups.
- **Secondary**: CFOs and Finance Operations (FinOps) leads looking for "low-hanging fruit" in cost cutting.

## Channels

### 1. Organic / Product-Led
- **LinkedIn**: Share "AI Spend Benchmark" reports (anonymized data from audits) to drive viral awareness.
- **Product Hunt**: Launch as a free "Tool of the Week" to capture a large initial pool of leads.

### 2. Community Outreach
- Post in Slack/Discord communities for CTOs (e.g., CTO Connection, Rands Leadership).
- Offer "Free AI Audit Workshops" for YC/startup batches.

### 3. SEO
- Target long-tail keywords: "OpenAI team plan vs plus", "optimize AI costs for startups", "GitHub Copilot enterprise pricing audit".

## Conversion Funnel
1. **Awareness**: Social post or SEO link.
2. **Value**: User completes audit in < 2 minutes.
3. **Lead Capture**: User enters email to get the "Full 12-page PDF Report".
4. **Sales**: Credex team follows up with personalized AI implementation consulting.
