- **Pain Point**: "I have 50 developers on GitHub Copilot and 20 on Cursor. I don't know who is using what and if we're paying twice for the same developer."
- **Feedback on Tool**: "The seat overlap check is exactly what I need. If you could integrate with Okta to automate this, I'd pay for it."

## Interview 2: Engineering Manager, AI Startup
- **Pain Point**: "Our OpenAI API bill jumped from $2k to $8k last month. I need a way to see if we're over-using expensive models for simple tasks."
- **Feedback on Tool**: "The recommendation to switch to GPT-4o-mini for 80% of tasks is a great rule of thumb. It gives me a defensible number to take to my boss."

## Interview 3: Finance Lead, E-commerce
- **Pain Point**: "We have dozens of 'Pro' subscriptions on personal credit cards. It's a nightmare for accounting."
- **Feedback on Tool**: "The 'Consolidate to Team Plan' advice is perfect. It's not just about cost; it's about control."
